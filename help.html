Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new invoice?

Go to Invoice → Create Invoice button. Fill in all details and submit.

Where does GSTIN appear in PDF?

Company GSTIN automatically appears at the top of the "Total in Words" box in every invoice style.

PDF resolution / quality?

PDF is generated at 300 DPI on exact A4 (210×297mm) with safe margins. Print-ready quality.
